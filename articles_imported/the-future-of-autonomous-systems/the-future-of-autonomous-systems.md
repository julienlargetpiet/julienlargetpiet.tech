## What are Autonomous Systems?

Autonomous systems are designed to operate independently, making decisions based on real-time data and sensor inputs. They can be found in various forms, including drones, self-driving cars, and robots. These systems are often equipped with sophisticated algorithms that enable them to learn and adapt to new situations.

## The Current State of Autonomous Systems

Currently, autonomous systems are being used in various industries, including transportation, logistics, and manufacturing. Self-driving cars are being tested on public roads, while drones are being used for package delivery and aerial surveying. Robots are being used in warehouses and factories to streamline production processes.
